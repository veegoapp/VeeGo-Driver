Booking rules to enforce in the UI:

Driver must book a full week (Sunday to Thursday) — no single-day booking
weekStart must always be a Sunday and at least the upcoming week
A booked timeslot must appear as unavailable to other drivers

Renewal flow in the UI:

Every Wednesday the driver receives a push notification with category: "shuttle_renewal" and a bookingId + deadlineIso
Show the driver a banner/modal telling them they have priority to renew — with a countdown to the deadline
If they confirm → call POST /api/shuttle/route-bookings/:id/confirm-renewal
If they miss the deadline → show them the slot is now open to others
