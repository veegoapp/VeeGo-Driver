- DELIVERY: On-demand — coming soon

Car/Scooter/Delivery must show as "Coming Soon".

### 7. SOCKET / REAL-TIME

- Where is socket initialized? (on app start? on login? on home screen?)

- Which socket events does the app LISTEN to? List each with handler
- Which socket events does the app EMIT? List each
- Is driver location broadcasting implemented? 
  (`driver:location:update` every X seconds while online)
- Is `driver:status:online` / `driver:status:offline` emitted on toggle?

- Are ride socket events handled?
  (`ride:offer`, `ride:offer_expired`, `ride:no_longer_available`)
- Any mock/fake socket events in the code?
